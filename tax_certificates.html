Tax Certificate Guide
About Tax Certificates

Tax certificates provide a summary of an employee's income and tax details for a specific financial year. These can be used for tax filing purposes or as proof of income.

Generating Individual Certificates
  1. Enter the regiment number of the employee.
  2. Select the financial year for which you want to generate the certificate.
  3. Click "Generate Certificate" to create the PDF certificate.
Batch Certificate Generation
  1. Select the financial year for all certificates.
  2. Choose the employees for whom you want to generate certificates. Leave empty to select all employees.
  3. Click "Generate Batch Certificates" to create multiple certificates.
Certificate Content

Each certificate includes:

  • Employee details (name, regiment number, designation, etc.)
  • Income breakdown (basic, allowances, gross income)
  • Tax calculation details
  • Bill claims and deductions if available
  • Digital signature of the authorized person

Note: Ensure that all salary data is correctly entered in the system before generating certificates to avoid any discrepancies.
